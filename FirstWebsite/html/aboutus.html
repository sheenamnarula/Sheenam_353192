Dreamland Dance Academy (DDA) is Bengaluru's finest dance academy for learning Latin dance forms such as Salsa, Zumba and Indian also. DDA is the brain child of Sheenam Narula, one of the country's foremost expert in Latin dance forms. DDA, started in the year 2006, with a sole intention of providing a state-of-the-art facility to learn the beautiful Latin dance forms which Bengaluru lacked, has trained over 15000 students. Sheenam Narula is the pioneer and founder of DDA, IIDC and GILF , 2 of the most famous dance festivals in ASIA She is the first person to host a Aisa Open Championship in a Non-Latin country.
